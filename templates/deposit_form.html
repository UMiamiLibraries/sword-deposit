Abstract

250 word limit. Please note that your text will get cut off if you reach the limit. Do not include the Abstract heading that appears in the manuscript. Do not paste directly from a Microsoft Word document. 'Save as' a text file, then copy/paste from the saved file.

Keywords

Six (6) keyword limit. Provide subject keywords or keyword phrases that will help others find your work. Think about what words people might use to search, and what the important concepts in your work are. Use sentence case for keywords (i.e. capitalize the first word of each keyword phrase, but not subsequent words). Spell out all acronyms. It would be beneficial to use a controlled list such as the Library of Congress Subject Headings
